Team Fortress 2

Price: Free

Rank: S

Description: Team Fortress 2 (TF2) is a popular, team-based first-person shooter developed by Valve Corporation. Released in 2007, it features nine distinct classes (e.g., Scout, Heavy, Engineer) that offer unique abilities and playstyles. The game emphasizes cooperation and strategy, with various modes like Capture the Flag, Control Points, and Payload. TF2 is known for its cartoony art style, humorous tone, and a thriving community that supports extensive modding and customization options.
